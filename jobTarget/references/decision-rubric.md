# Decision Rubric

Use this rubric only after the scorecard is complete enough.

## 建议冲

Choose this when most of the following are true:

- business potential is high
- company or product competitiveness is strong enough
- org and team signals are healthy
- role realism is high
- candidate fit is medium-to-high with closable gaps
- reward-to-risk ratio is attractive

## 可继续推进但需重点验证

Choose this when upside exists but a few key unknowns remain:

- business is interesting but org or team evidence is partial
- community sentiment is mixed or noisy
- role scope is promising but realism is not fully confirmed
- candidate fit is workable but one or two important gaps remain

This is the default outcome when evidence is promising but incomplete.

## 谨慎推进

Choose this when concerns are material but not disqualifying:

- org turbulence signs are visible
- title-to-scope mismatch is likely
- community signals repeatedly mention serious pressure or chaos
- candidate fit has multiple hard gaps
- opportunity cost is non-trivial

## 不建议

Choose this when downside is dominant:

- business appears weak or de-prioritized
- role realism is low
- org risk is high and supported by multiple signals
- candidate fit is poor
- the likely upside does not justify the switching risk

## Recommendation writing rule

Always mention:

- the strongest reason to proceed
- the strongest reason to hesitate
- the next verification action
